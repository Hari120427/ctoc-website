Our Story

How CTOC Started

It started with a simple observation — talented students with real potential were struggling not because of lack of skills, but because they didn't know where to start.

High coaching fees, confusing processes, and zero guidance were stopping deserving students from landing their first job. That's when Harikaran L decided to build something different.

Code to Career was born with one mission — make career guidance affordable, accessible, and beginner-friendly for every student, regardless of their background or budget.
